FAQs

Will the data on my computer be safe?

We completely wipe the storage drive using DBAN. If you are still concerned, we can physically remove the drive from the computer for you to keep.

Can I get tax credit/exemption for my donation?

The Power Up Project is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. We will provide a receipt upon request.

Will you accept...?

  • Printers/Scanners: No
  • Fax Machines: No
  • Mobile Phones: Yes
  • Gaming Consoles (Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo): Yes, if they are in working condition
  • Tablets/iPads: Yes
  • Laptops: Yes, even if they are broken or outdated
  • PCs: Yes, even if they are broken or outdated
  • Computer Parts (RAM, SSDs, Hard Drives, Power Supplies, etc.): Yes
  • Computer Accessories (Mice, Keyboards, Cables, etc.): Yes
  • Flatscreen Monitors: Yes, if they are in working condition. (We do NOT accept CRT monitors)
